Museum of Ice Cream Drawing App
museum installation

‍

Commisioned by The Museum of Ice Cream, I developed a site specific iPad/Mac drawing app, now showing in Austin and Singapore.

‍

‍

Using the app, museum guests can make their own artwork, then see it displayed as a framed work of art on the wall.

‍

‍

I developed both the Mac and iPad apps using Xcode and SwiftUI, doing 100% of the engineering myself. The biggest design challenge was making the interactions instantly understandable across cultures. The biggest technical challenge was making the iPads and Macs talk to each other over the network and show the right drawing on the right screen at the right time.

It was a fun project and I'm glad to have contributed to the Museum of Ice Cream!
