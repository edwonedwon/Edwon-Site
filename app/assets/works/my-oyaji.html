A generational story about identity, distance, and repair.

This page is a working outline for the upcoming film. It is structured to match the rest of the portfolio while keeping unreleased details private.

Logline

An emotionally grounded family drama that follows a son confronting unresolved history with his father while navigating the push and pull between cultures, duty, and self-expression.

Visual Direction

Intimate framing, textured real-world locations, and stylized memory transitions. The look balances realism with poetic visual beats to emphasize emotional perspective.

Themes

Father-son distance, inherited expectations, diaspora identity, vulnerability, and the difficult work of reconciliation.
