Wuboz
NFT collection

‍

The Wuboz are a collection of randomly generated NFT characters sold and traded on FXHash.

Each and every Wuboz is completely unique and generated in realtime on the Tezos blockchain. When you mint, a new Wuboz is instantly generated based on a unique hashtag.

I coded this project using Javascript and the wonderful ZDog library by David DeSandro.

Wuboz are sold on FXHash here:

‍https://www.fxhash.xyz/generative/slug/wuboz
